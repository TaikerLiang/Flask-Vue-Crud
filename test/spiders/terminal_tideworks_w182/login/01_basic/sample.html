TRUCK DRIVER SAFETY IN THE TERMINAL

Pacific Container Terminal (PCT), Pier J, Long Beach, CA, is committed to providing a safe working environment for all workers at our facility. As part of this effort, we would like to remind all truck drivers of the following basic safety requirements: 

1. Upon entering the terminal, all drivers MUSTwear a high visibility Safety Vest.

2.  Truck drivers MUST remain in their vehicle at all times while in the container yard. (The only exception is hooking up or disconnecting a chassis).

3.  Truck drivers must NEVER approach operating equipment under any circumstances.

Failure to follow these safety rules will result in loss of terminal access privileges.  Please be sure to advise any/all drivers you may have working at SSA Terminals.

Please remind your drivers of the following safety rule to help keep them safe.

Stay back at least one container length when Toppick/Sidepick is in a stacking row retrieving your container.  Move forward only after Toppick/Sidepick completely backs out of row.


Thank you for your cooperation,

PCT Management
