B/L numbershould start with ZIMU followed by 3 A/N letter followed by 6-9 numbers (for example ZIMUBCN123456).
Abooking numbermust have A/N characters only.
Acontainer numbermust have 4 A/N followed by 6 to 7 digits (for example ZCSU1234567).
