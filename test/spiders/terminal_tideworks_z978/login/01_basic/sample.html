TRUCK DRIVER SAFETY

 
SSA Terminals- Pier A, Long Beach, is committed to providing a safe working environment for all workers at our facility.  As part of this effort, we would like to remind all truck drivers of the following basic safety requirements:


1. Upon entering the terminal, all drivers MUST wear a high visibility safety vest.

2. Truck drivers MUST remain in their vehicle at all times while in the container yard. (The only exception is hooking up or disconnecting a chassis).

3. Drivers must NEVER approach operating equipment under any circumstances!


Failure to follow these safety rules will result in loss of terminal access privileges. Please be sure to advise any/all drivers you may have working at SSA Terminals.
